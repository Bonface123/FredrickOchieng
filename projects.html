Electricity Consumption Prediction for Tétouan, Morocco

Project Details

This project applies time series analysis to forecast electricity consumption in 10-minute intervals for the city of Tétouan, Morocco. By providing accurate predictions, the project supports efficient energy management for the city's 380,000 residents.

Key Features:

  1. Data Processing: Analyzed 500,000+ data points to identify energy usage patterns, resulting in a 20% improvement in prediction accuracy.
  2. Predictive Modeling: Developed and optimized three predictive models to forecast demand, reducing error rates by 15%.
  3. Interactive Dashboards: Designed five dashboards for real-time monitoring, empowering stakeholders with data-driven insights and improving decision-making efficiency.
  4. This project equips city planners and utility managers with actionable insights to manage energy resources effectively, addressing demand fluctuations and supporting sustainable urban infrastructure.

Power BI Sales Dashboard

Project Details

This Power BI Sales Dashboard offers a comprehensive analysis of sales data from 2018 to 2021, designed to provide valuable insights into business performance.

Key Features:

  1. Monthly Sales Trends: A dynamic line chart that visualizes monthly quantities sold, helping users easily identify trends and seasonal patterns.
  2. Customer Insights: A bar graph illustrates total sales by customer category, helping businesses understand which segments contribute most to revenue.
  3. Geographical Analysis: An interactive map displays sales distribution across various regions, offering a clearer picture of location-based performance.
  4. Custom Filters: Users can explore specific time frames or categories through customizable filters, enhancing data exploration and analysis.

This user-friendly dashboard can be tailored by connecting to your own data source in Power BI Desktop. Contributions to further enhance its functionality are welcome.

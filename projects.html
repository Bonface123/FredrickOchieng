Electricity Consumption Forecasting for Tétouan, Morocco

Project Overview

This project leverages time series analysis to forecast electricity consumption in 10-minute intervals for Tétouan, Morocco. By analyzing high-resolution data and key weather parameters, the project aims to optimize energy resource planning and support the integration of renewable energy sources.

Context

Tétouan, a northern Moroccan city with a population of 380,000, experiences fluctuating energy demands influenced by its Mediterranean climate. With energy production primarily reliant on non-renewable sources (64%), accurate demand forecasting is essential for improving energy efficiency and planning.

The city’s power distribution network, managed by Amendis, is divided into three zones: Quads, Smir, and Boussafou. Each zone’s unique consumption patterns provide an opportunity to design localized solutions for energy management.

Dataset

The dataset, collected by Amendis’ SCADA system, contains 52,416 observations recorded at 10-minute intervals from January 1 to December 30, 2017. It includes:

  • Weather Variables: Temperature (°C), humidity (%), and wind speed (km/h).
  • Energy Consumption: Zone-wise power usage (kW) for three distinct areas.

Key Highlights

  1. High-Resolution Insights: The granularity of 10-minute intervals enables precise forecasting.
  2. Zone-Specific Analysis: Consumption patterns for each zone reveal localized trends.
  3. Weather Impact Assessment: Examines the role of environmental factors in energy usage.

Objectives

  1. Predict Electricity Demand: Anticipate fluctuations in consumption across Tétouan’s zones.
  2. Support Renewable Integration: Facilitate effective use of solar and wind energy.
  3. Enable Better Resource Planning: Assist stakeholders in optimizing energy procurement and distribution.

2023 Florida Medicaid Report: Analyzing Humana’s Market Presence

Project Details

This project provides an in-depth analysis of Florida's Medicaid program, focusing on Humana’s market share as of March 2023. By leveraging a comprehensive healthcare dataset, the analysis offers valuable insights into Humana’s membership distribution across Florida’s counties.

Key Features:

  1. Interactive Map Visualization: A map displays Humana's Medicaid market share by county, allowing users to explore regional variations and identify areas with the largest and smallest shares.
  2. Slicer Functionality: A county slicer enables users to filter data dynamically by specific county names, making the data more accessible and customizable.
  3. Summary Cards: Key performance indicators are highlighted using cards, showing:
    • Total Humana Members: 813,254
    • Total Medicaid Members: 4,524,170
    • Overall Market Share: 18%

This report serves as a critical tool for healthcare providers, policymakers, and analysts looking to make data-driven decisions in Medicaid management.

Power BI Sales Dashboard

Project Details

This Power BI Sales Dashboard offers a comprehensive analysis of sales data from 2018 to 2021, designed to provide valuable insights into business performance.

Key Features:

This user-friendly dashboard can be tailored by connecting to your own data source in Power BI Desktop. Contributions to further enhance its functionality are welcome.

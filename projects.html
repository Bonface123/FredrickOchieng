Power BI Sales Dashboard

Project Details

This Power BI Sales Dashboard offers a comprehensive analysis of sales data from 2018 to 2021, designed to provide valuable insights into business performance.

Key Features:

  1. Monthly Sales Trends: A dynamic line chart that visualizes monthly quantities sold, helping users easily identify trends and seasonal patterns.
  2. Customer Insights: A bar graph illustrates total sales by customer category, helping businesses understand which segments contribute most to revenue.
  3. Geographical Analysis: An interactive map displays sales distribution across various regions, offering a clearer picture of location-based performance.
  4. Custom Filters: Users can explore specific time frames or categories through customizable filters, enhancing data exploration and analysis.

This user-friendly dashboard can be tailored by connecting to your own data source in Power BI Desktop. Contributions to further enhance its functionality are welcome.
